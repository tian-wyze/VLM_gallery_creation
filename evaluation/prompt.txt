- 1 query image (labelled "Query:")

- The same person may be wearing different clothes across images — do NOT totally rely on clothing or accessories as primary matching criteria.
- Focus exclusively on person-specific biometric features: body shape and build, facial features, hair style and colour, skin tone, and overall physical appearance.
- At most ONE image option can match the query. If the query person is not shown in any image option, the correct answer is the letter of the "stranger" option.

Output format (respond with EXACTLY this format and nothing else):
"Answer: X."
where X is a single letter naming the matching option (A, B, C, ...) — either the letter of the gallery image that matches the query, or the letter of the "stranger" option if no image matches.
